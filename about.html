About Our Website


We, humans, grief in varies ways. Creating a memorial to remember someone we loved after they have passed away is one way of doing so. By creating a memorial, you can always have a way to remember those you've lost. Especially during this time of social distancing, a virtual way to remember your loved one is a safer way for everyone.
